About Us


Food Network is a famous food court in Bangladesh. Our main goal is to serve healthy food. We started our journey in 2015. In a short time we have gained a lot of reputation. We have a total of 6 branches across the country.

In addition to the quality of food, we are paying special attention to the environment of our court. We ensure a relaxing, neat and clean environment.
